# ABE MUSIC — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: ABE MUSIC — THE HUB
- **Type**: Record label platform
- **Tone**: Premium, gold standard, authoritative

## Colors
- Primary: #FFD700 (Gold)
- Secondary: #1A1A2E (Dark Navy)
- Accent: #E94560 (Striking Red)
- Background: #0F0F23 (Deep Navy)
- Surface: rgba(255,215,0,0.05)
- Text: #F0F0F0 (Bright White)

## Typography
- Display: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif (weight 800 headings)
- Body: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif

## Spacing
- Base: 8px
- Scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96

## Components
- Cards: border-radius 12px, gold accent border
- Buttons: gradient gold-to-red, dark text for contrast
- Stats: large gold numbers, muted labels
