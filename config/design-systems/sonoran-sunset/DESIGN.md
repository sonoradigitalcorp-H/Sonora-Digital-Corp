# Sonoran Sunset — Design System

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Sonoran Sunset (AzREC)
- **Type**: Creative brand / Music label
- **Tone**: Bold, warm, desert-inspired

## Colors
- Primary: #FF6B35 (Sunset Orange)
- Secondary: #FFD700 (Gold)
- Accent: #E94560 (Vibrant Red)
- Background: #1A0A00 (Deep Desert)
- Surface: rgba(255,107,53,0.05)
- Text: #F5F0EB (Warm White)

## Typography
- Display: 'Playfair Display', serif (headings)
- Body: 'Inter', system-ui, sans-serif
- Monospace: 'JetBrains Mono', monospace

## Spacing
- Base: 8px
- Scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96

## Components
- Cards: border-radius 16px, warm border glow
- Buttons: gradient sunset, bold weight
- Nav: transparent, glassmorphism on scroll
