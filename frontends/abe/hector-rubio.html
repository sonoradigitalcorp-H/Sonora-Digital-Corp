Biography

Hector Rubio began his musical journey in 2017 in Angostura, Sinaloa, composing his first song "Ya Me Enteré", performed by Jovanny Cadena. After releasing his debut EP "Así Fue El Muchacho" in 2020, he made the move to Culiacán, Sinaloa in 2021, connecting with the region's most influential corrido figures.

His breakthrough came as a composer for Alex Favela, writing hits like those on the "Eloverga" and "Alvrgazo" projects. His composition "Ahí Les Encargo" (performed by Edgardo Núñez & Julian Mercado) amassed over 6 million Spotify streams and 18 million YouTube views.

In 2024, Hector reached a career milestone when Peso Pluma invited him to join the "Éxodo" US tour, performing at iconic venues including T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas and dates across California (Sacramento, San Francisco, San Diego, Palm Springs, Fresno).

January 2025 saw the release of his album "SMOKING" (7 tracks), featuring the hit single "Poker Star". Released under ABE Music Inc and VIZUAL, the album represents his most mature work to date, blending traditional corridos with modern production.
