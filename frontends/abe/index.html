About ABE Music Group

Founded in Los Angeles with a mission to elevate Regional Mexican music to the global stage through technology.

ABE Music Inc was founded in January 2018 by Abraham S. Ortega in Compton, California. What began as a traditional record label has evolved into a music technology group combining artist development with AI-powered platforms.

Our artists span Regional Mexicano, Corridos, Sierreño, and Latin Pop. We’ve grown to manage 120M+ streams and $479K+ in tracked revenue through our partnership with Oplaai Music and Colonize Media.

Through Sonora Digital Corp, we’ve built the ABE Music OS — an AI-native operating system for independent labels that combines distribution, revenue intelligence, content automation, and fan engagement in one platform.
