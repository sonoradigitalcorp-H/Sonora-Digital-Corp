About ABE Music

Founded in Los Angeles with a mission to elevate Regional Mexican music to the global stage.

ABE Music Inc was founded in January 2018 by Abraham S. Ortega with a vision to discover, develop, and promote authentic Latin music talent. Based in Compton, California, our label sits at the crossroads of traditional Mexican music and modern urban influence.

We represent artists across Regional Mexicano, Corridos, Sierreño, and Latin Pop genres. Our roster includes Hector Rubio (1.2M+ monthly listeners, opened for Peso Pluma's Éxodo Tour), Jesus Urquijo, and Javier Arvayo.

Through our partnership with Oplaai Music and distribution networks across the US and Mexico, we deliver real results for our artists — from studio to stage to streaming.
