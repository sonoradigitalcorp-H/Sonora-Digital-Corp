Biography

Francisco Javier Arvayo Sánchez, known professionally as Javier Arvayo, was born and raised in Hermosillo, Sonora, México. A passionate vocalist and accomplished guitarist, Javier blends Latin Pop with traditional Mexican influences to create a sound uniquely his own.

He was featured on ABE Music's debut compilation "Abe Music Presenta" (2020) alongside Roberto Amaya and Christian Ortega. His most recent work includes the live album "Mis Favoritas Con Tololoche (En Vivo)" (2025) and the singles "Los Tigres Mandan" and "Coco Psycho" (2026), the latter written by fellow ABE Music artist Jesus Urquijo.

With over 15 released songs and a growing presence on Instagram (20K followers), Javier continues to develop his craft and connect with audiences across the US and Mexico.
